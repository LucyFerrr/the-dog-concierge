Frequently Asked Questions

Here are common questions we receive at The Dog Concierge:

How often should I groom my dog?

The frequency of grooming depends on your dog's breed, coat type, and lifestyle. Generally, dogs with longer coats may require grooming every 4-6 weeks, while short-haired breeds may need grooming every 8-12 weeks.

How do I brush my dog's teeth?

Use a dog-specific toothbrush and toothpaste. Gently lift your dog's lips and brush in a circular motion. Start slowly to get your dog accustomed to the process, and aim to brush their teeth several times a week.

What is the best way to train my puppy?

Consistency, positive reinforcement, and patience are key. Start with basic commands like sit, stay, and come. Reward good behavior with treats and praise, and consider enrolling in a puppy training class for additional support.
